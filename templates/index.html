How to Protect Yourself

Precaution dose

Now Precaution dose for 18-59 age group free at Government Vaccination Centre.

Maintain social distancing

Keep distance between yourself and others when you're in indoor public spaces. This is especially important if you have a higher risk of serious illness.

Wash your hands frequently

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, or use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol.

Avoid touching eyes,nose and mouth

Cover your mouth and nose with your elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze. Throw away the used tissue. Wash your hands right away.
